Project Overview

WhichWayNigeria is a civic engagement platform that empowers citizens to explore electoral data, verify voter registration, engage with political discourse, and track the evolving pulse of Nigeria's democratic journey.

Problem Definition

Problem

Staying informed about Nigeria's electoral process is scattered across platforms, leaving citizens without a unified, reliable space for civic engagement and voter education.

Solution

WhichWayNigeria delivers real-time electoral data discovery, transparent candidate information, and community connection in one seamless platform.

PVC Verification

Instant voter registration status checking with INEC database integration.

Polling Unit Locator

Find your exact voting location using GPS and ward mapping technology.

Candidate Profiles

Comprehensive ADC candidate information and manifesto comparisons.
